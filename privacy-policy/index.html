Privacy Policy - Senior Nutrition App

Last Updated: August 2025

1. Introduction

Senior Nutrition App ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Health Information

  • Nutritional data (meals, food intake, dietary preferences)
  • Medication information (names, dosages, schedules)
  • Health metrics (weight, blood pressure, heart rate, blood sugar)
  • Fasting periods and schedules
  • Water intake tracking
  • Medical appointments and reminders
  • AI interaction data for personalized nutrition recommendations
  • Voice input data for hands-free meal and medication logging

2.2 App Usage Information

  • App preferences and settings
  • Language preferences
  • Notification preferences
  • Voice settings

2.3 Device Information

  • Device type and operating system
  • App version information
  • Crash reports and error logs

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide personalized nutrition and health tracking
  • Generate AI-powered meal suggestions and nutrition advice
  • Send medication and meal reminders
  • Generate health reports and analytics
  • Export your data in PDF or CSV formats (Premium feature)
  • Provide medical citations and references for health information
  • Support voice-activated features for accessibility
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Provide customer support

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

Your health data is primarily stored locally on your device using Core Data. This means your sensitive information stays on your device and is not automatically shared with external servers.

4.2 Optional Cloud Sync

If you choose to enable CloudKit sync, your data may be stored in Apple's iCloud service. This is entirely optional and controlled by you. Apple's privacy policies apply to any data stored in iCloud.

4.3 Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encryption of sensitive data
  • Secure data transmission protocols
  • Regular security assessments
  • Access controls and authentication

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal health information with third parties.

Limited sharing may occur only in these circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent
  • When required by law
  • To protect our rights or safety
  • With Apple (for iCloud sync, if enabled)

6. Premium Features and Subscription Model

Our app uses a freemium business model with premium features available through in-app purchases:

  • Free Features: Basic nutrition tracking, water monitoring, medication reminders, general health tips
  • Premium Features: AI-powered nutrition advice, personalized meal suggestions, advanced analytics, data export (PDF/CSV), priority support
  • Subscription Options: Monthly ($9.99/month) or Annual ($99.99/year) with 7-day free trial

Purchase information is handled by Apple's App Store and is subject to Apple's privacy policies. We do not store your payment information.

7. Third-Party Services and AI Features

Our app may integrate with:

  • Apple's notification system
  • Apple's voice recognition services
  • Local AI processing for nutrition recommendations (runs on-device)
  • Medical and nutritional databases for citations and references

Apple services are governed by Apple's privacy policies. Our AI features process data locally on your device when possible to maintain privacy.

7.1 Medical Citations and References

We provide medical citations from reputable sources including:

  • National Institutes of Health (NIH)
  • American Heart Association (AHA)
  • U.S. Preventive Services Task Force
  • Peer-reviewed medical journals

These references are provided for educational purposes and link to external websites governed by their respective privacy policies.

8. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your data
  • Export your data (CSV/PDF format available with Premium subscription)
  • Disable cloud sync
  • Control notification preferences
  • Manage subscription and billing through Apple's App Store
  • Disable AI features and recommendations
  • Control voice input permissions

9. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as you use the app. You can delete your data at any time through the app settings or by uninstalling the app.

10. Children's Privacy

Our app is designed for seniors (ages 50+) and is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

11. International Users and Localization

Our app is available in multiple languages (English, Spanish, French, Hebrew) and regions. Data protection laws may vary by location. We comply with applicable privacy laws in the jurisdictions where our app is available, including:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for European users
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) for California residents
  • Other applicable regional privacy regulations

All privacy notices and controls are available in your preferred language through the app's language settings.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date.

13. Medical Disclaimer and Health Information

Important: This app is for tracking and informational purposes only. It is not intended to provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult with healthcare professionals for medical decisions.

Medical Citations: We provide medical citations and references from reputable sources to support health information presented in the app. These citations are for educational purposes only and should not replace professional medical advice. All health tips and recommendations include proper medical citations when applicable.

AI-Generated Content: AI-powered nutrition suggestions are based on general nutritional guidelines and your provided information. They are not personalized medical advice and should not replace consultation with qualified healthcare providers.
