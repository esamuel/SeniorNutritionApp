Privacy Policy - Senior Nutrition App

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

Senior Nutrition App ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Health Information

  • Nutritional data (meals, food intake, dietary preferences)
  • Medication information (names, dosages, schedules)
  • Health metrics (weight, blood pressure, heart rate, blood sugar)
  • Fasting periods and schedules
  • Water intake tracking
  • Medical appointments and reminders

2.2 App Usage Information

  • App preferences and settings
  • Language preferences
  • Notification preferences
  • Voice settings

2.3 Device Information

  • Device type and operating system
  • App version information
  • Crash reports and error logs

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide personalized nutrition and health tracking
  • Send medication and meal reminders
  • Generate health reports and analytics
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Provide customer support

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

Your health data is primarily stored locally on your device using Core Data. This means your sensitive information stays on your device and is not automatically shared with external servers.

4.2 Optional Cloud Sync

If you choose to enable CloudKit sync, your data may be stored in Apple's iCloud service. This is entirely optional and controlled by you. Apple's privacy policies apply to any data stored in iCloud.

4.3 Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encryption of sensitive data
  • Secure data transmission protocols
  • Regular security assessments
  • Access controls and authentication

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal health information with third parties.

Limited sharing may occur only in these circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent
  • When required by law
  • To protect our rights or safety
  • With Apple (for iCloud sync, if enabled)

6. Premium Features

Our app includes premium features available through in-app purchases. Purchase information is handled by Apple's App Store and is subject to Apple's privacy policies. We do not store your payment information.

7. Third-Party Services

Our app may integrate with:

  • Apple HealthKit (with your permission)
  • Apple's notification system
  • Apple's voice recognition services

These services are governed by Apple's privacy policies.

8. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your data
  • Export your data
  • Disable cloud sync
  • Control notification preferences

9. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as you use the app. You can delete your data at any time through the app settings or by uninstalling the app.

10. Children's Privacy

Our app is designed for seniors (ages 50+) and is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

11. International Users

Our app is available in multiple languages and regions. Data protection laws may vary by location. We comply with applicable privacy laws in the jurisdictions where our app is available.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date.

13. Medical Disclaimer

Important: This app is for tracking and informational purposes only. It is not intended to provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult with healthcare professionals for medical decisions.
